{{Documentation subpage}}
This infobox is designed to put basic factual information, an image and optionally a map in an organized fashion for an 'urban feature',
which is essentially anything from a public square to a public staircase, into an infobox. The infobox helps to give details to the reader in a straightforward standard manner.

==Korean transcriptions==
To add Korean name transcriptions, use {{tl|Infobox Korean name}} with {{para|child|yes}} (see, for example, [[#Example 3|Example 3]]).

==Example articles==
Example articles include [[Potemkin Stairs]], [[Nathan Phillips Square]], [[Yonge-Dundas Square]] and [[Baldwin Steps]].

=== Example 1 ===
{{Infobox urban feature
|name=Nathan Phillips Square
|image_place=Toronto - ON - Rathaus und Nathan Phillips Square.jpg
|image_dot_map          = To-locator-map.png
|dot_mapsize            = 275px
|dot_map_caption        = Location of Nathan Phillips Square in Toronto
|dot_x =112   |dot_y =91
|coordinates_display= inline
| latd = 43 | latm = 39 | lats = 9 | latNS = N
| longd = 79 | longm = 23 | longs = 1 | longEW = W
|location=Bay Street and Queen Street
|address2=[[Toronto]], [[Ontario|ON]], [[Canada]]
|place_type=City square
|owner=City of Toronto
|designer=[[Viljo Revell]]
|open=1965
}}
