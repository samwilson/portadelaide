==Purpose==

The template is for making external links to the [http://avibase.bsc-eoc.org Avibase] ornithology taxonomy database.

==Format==
All parameters are '''required.'''

 <nowiki>{{</nowiki>avibase|avibase id|free text<nowiki>}}</nowiki>

The Avibase ID can be found in the search results for that species. (Note this is ''not'' the same as the Taxomic Serial Number).
==Example of usage==

 <nowiki>*{{</nowiki>avibase|1BB50DE8362A4963|Turdus merula<nowiki>}}</nowiki>

will be displayed as

*{{avibase|1BB50DE8362A4963|Turdus merula}}
