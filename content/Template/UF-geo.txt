The HTML mark up produced by this template includes an [[Geo (microformat)| Geo microformat]], which makes the location's [[Geographic coordinate system|coordinates]] (latitude & longitude) [[Parsing|parsable]], so that they can be, say, looked up on a map, or downloaded to a [[Global Positioning System|GPS]] unit. For more information about the use of [[microformat]]s on Wikipedia, please see [[WP:UF|the microformat project]].

Geo is usually produced by calling {{tl|coord}}, and uses HTML classes:

{{Flatlist/microformat|
*geo
*latitude
*longitude
}}

'''Please do not rename or remove these classes.'''

When giving coordinates, please don't be [[Wikipedia:WikiProject Geographical coordinates#Precision|overly precise]].
