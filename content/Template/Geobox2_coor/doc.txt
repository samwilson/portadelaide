This template is transcluded by:
*[[Template:Geobox]]

It calls {{tl|coord}} to display coordinates.

==Sections of Geobox calling this template ==

Main fields:
<pre>
| lat_d = | lat_m = | lat_s = | lat_NS = 
| long_d = | long_m = | long_s = | long_EW =
</pre>
Sometimes used:
<pre>
| coordinates_format =
| coordinates_type =
| coordinates_note =
</pre>
Other sets of fields with the following prefixes:
* capital_
* highest_
* lowest_
* mouth_
* source_
* source1_
* source_confluence_
* management_
* government_
etc.
