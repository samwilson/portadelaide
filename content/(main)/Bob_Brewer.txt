Robert (Bob) BREWER, 
:Name added to the west side of the Workers Memorial in 1998
:He was nominated by the CFMEU

Robert Brewer raised seven children in New South Wales, New Zealand and then settled in Adelaide.

He was a worker for working people, the poor, the indigenous and unemployed.  Robert worked as an Engine Driver and plant operator and volunteered as Treasurer of the Semaphore Workers Club.
