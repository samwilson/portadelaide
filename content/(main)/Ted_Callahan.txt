Edward (Ted) John Callahan

:Name added to north side of the Workers Memorial between 1980 - 1984
:He was nominated by   

Edward (Ted) John Callaghan was a Port Adelaide Councillor for West Ward from 1977 -1978.  He died in 1979

Edward was a local West Ward resident of long standing, with a proven record of interest in local affairs.  He sought funding to develop roads in the West Ward of Port Adelaide claiming the area was the Cinderella of the Port, and to improve the Semaphore shopping precinct.

West Ward residents were welcome to contact Ted at home and said he would not encumber himself with outside matters and would always make Council duties his first priority.
Messenger Newspaper June 19 1977 p7 profile
