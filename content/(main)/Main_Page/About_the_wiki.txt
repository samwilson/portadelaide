==MediaWiki versions==
===Initial startup of the wiki===
The wiki was created on a MediaWiki 1.23 platform on 17 May 2015, and later in 2015 upgraded to MediaWiki 1.23.11.

===Upgrades===
* MediaWiki 1.26.2 - 29 December 2016.

==Hosting==
The wiki was originally hosted on a commercial webserver in the U.S.A., but this proved to be too expensive.

In July 2017 the wiki was migrated to a local, Adelaide-based server, hosted by the Australian Public Access Network Association Inc., ([http://adelaide.apana.org.au/ APANA]), a not-for-profit community group.
