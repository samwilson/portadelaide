===Port Adelaide Library:===
Daniel Fletcher

West Side

Name added to memorial 1918

He was nominated by the Amalgamated Society of Carpenters and Joiners

Daniel, a carpenter, was born in Bradford, England 1860 and immigrated to South Australia in 1881. He was awarded a Hymn Christian Worship book on his final day at Sunday School in England.

Trustee of the Amalgamated Society of Carpenters and Joiners 

Delegate to the Trades and Labour Council for the above

He married Jane Vernon; they had four children.  

It is said he worked on the staircase of the Largs Pier Hotel.
